What is Vacation Planner?

Today, when planning a vacation, there are numerous things to consider. Hotel packages, flight times, restaurant suggestions and potential attractions are all scattered among the web on sites such as Triposo, Amadeus, and MediaWiki. What our teams wants to allow for is an easier method of vacation planning, by combining APIs from different travel agencies.

This will help individuals plan their first vacation if he or she is new to trip planning, as well as allow for experienced individuals to find the options their destination has to offer in a more efficient manner. Because costs are a big factor in which destinations a vacation-goer chooses to travel to, our vacation planner will be able to find the most suitable and cost-effective vacation based on the travellerâ€™s budget preferences.

Although the Internet has made it possible for the average person to conveniently plan a trip, it has also made it much more complicated due to the abundance of choice it provides. The sheer number of flights and hotel options available today leave people wondering where to even begin searching. The goal of our application is to simplify the process of planning a trip so that a person with any level of computer skill is able to successfully plan a trip for themselves and others in a quick, easy, and cost-effective manner.

How to Use

1: Pick your flight.

Go to the Flights tab and choose from the options on that page. On the Flights page, you can choose which city you're leaving from and where you're going to. You can choose a departure and return date as well as picking a budget for your flight. The filter options inlcude different airlines, flight type, and number of stops.

2: Pick your housing.

Go to the Housing tab and choose from the options on that page. On the Housing page, you can enter the city are visiting, the check in/out dates, and pick a budget. The filter options include number of guests, number of rooms, max distance from your current location, and the ratings of the hotels.

3: Pick your points of interest.

Go to the Point of Interest tab and choose from the options on that page. The filter options include picking a destination, restaurant, attraction, and transportation.

Accessibility Statement

For users with color-blindness or motor impairments:

The Vacation Planner team is dedicated to delivering a quality and enjoyable experience to all users. We have worked, and are continuing to work, to educate ourselves about disabilities such as motor impairments and color-blindness conditions. Because of this, we have designed our website to use a color palatte which provides a fun and colorful experience for users with any type of color blindness.

In addition to this, we have designed our website and undergone user tests to ensure that our button sizes are acceptable and provide for ease of use.

We hope that you will enjoy using our website.
